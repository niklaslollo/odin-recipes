Oatmeal

Description

This is an amazing recipe that will nourish you in the morning.

You are going to be so ready to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Oats - half cup
  • Banana - one
  • Cinnamon - one tsp
  • Milk - one cup

Steps

  1. Put Oats in a bowl
  2. Slice the banana into ten slices
  3. Put Banana in bowl
  4. Add milk
  5. Sprinkle on cinnamon
