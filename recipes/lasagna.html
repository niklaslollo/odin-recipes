Lasagna

Description

This is an amazing recipe that will delight you and your family.

You are going to be so full!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta - one box
  • Pasta sauce - 16 oz jar
  • Good cheese - one bag shredded

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna pasta
  2. Line the baking dish with the cooked pasta
  3. Layer on sauce and cheese
  4. Layer on more pasta
  5. Continuing with the previous steps until you run out of ingredients
